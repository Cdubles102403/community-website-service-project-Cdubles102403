Studying Tips

  • Give yourself enough time to study, Start studying for a test the day you here about the test.
  • Use an app like EverNote to keep your notes organized.
  • Use drawings and flow charts to give yourself a visual.
  • Take breaks while studying, 30 minutes of studying followed by a 10 minute break is proven to work well.
  • Keep your study space organized and free from distractions such as phones.
  • Study with a group from your class.
